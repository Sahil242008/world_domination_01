How to Play

Objective

Conquer the world by capturing territories until you control 75% of the world or eliminate all opponents.

Game Flow

  1. Each turn, you collect resources based on territories you control
  2. Build armies to strengthen your territories
  3. Attack adjacent territories to expand your empire
  4. Research technologies to gain advantages
  5. End your turn to allow other players to act

Combat

To attack, select your territory, then click an adjacent enemy territory. The territory with more army strength has a higher chance of winning.
